About me_

I am a communication & multimedia student at University of Applied Sciences of Amsterdam. But I would like to call myself a Visual designer to be more specific. I Love working with graphic design and typography, even more when I can use it to make user interfaces! I am currently building this website from the ground up with HTML, CSS and JS. It will probably change over time since this started as an expirement and learning project. The goal of my portfolio is to show what type of style I like and what i would like to identify with.
